Methodology

How the optimisation works

CARBOMICA adapts the ATOMICA resource-allocation framework for healthcare sustainability. Interventions are ranked by tCO₂e reduced per USD spent and selected greedily until the budget is exhausted. Three scenarios are compared side-by-side:

Full coverage All interventions — theoretical maximum reduction
Fixed budget Cheapest first — maximises count within budget
Optimised Greedy knapsack — max tCO₂e per dollar

Reference: HIGH Horizons D3.7 (Luchters et al., 2024, DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.12730527)
