Emission conversion methodology

Raw usage data (kWh, m³, kg, litres, km, units) is converted to tCO₂e using country-specific grid emission factors (IEA 2022) and GHG Protocol / DEFRA 2023 combustion factors.

Electricity factors: Zimbabwe 0.556 kg CO₂e/kWh (coal grid) · South Africa 0.928 kg CO₂e/kWh (Eskom) · Kenya 0.032 kg CO₂e/kWh (renewables-heavy). Anaesthetic gases use a weighted average GWP of 802 (50% isoflurane GWP 510, 30% sevoflurane GWP 130, 20% desflurane GWP 2540 — IPCC AR6).
