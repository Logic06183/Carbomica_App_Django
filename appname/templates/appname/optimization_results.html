Interpreting your results
tCO₂e reduction Tonnes of CO₂-equivalent avoided per year if the intervention is implemented. This is the primary impact metric.
ROI (10-year) Return on investment over 10 years relative to total implementation + maintenance cost. Positive = financially viable without carbon credit income.
Payback period Years until cumulative annual savings equal the upfront cost. Interventions under 5 years are considered strong performers in LMIC contexts.
SDG alignment Which UN Sustainable Development Goals this intervention contributes to — useful for grant reporting and policy narratives.
