Scientific basis & data provenance

Emissions are estimated from activity data (kWh, litres, kg, km) converted to tCO₂e using internationally standardised carbon factors: grid electricity — IEA Emissions Factors 2022 (country-specific); fuels, travel, waste, inhalers — UK DEFRA/BEIS 2023; anaesthetic & refrigerant gases — IPCC AR6 GWP₁₀₀. Optimisation ranks interventions by tCO₂e reduced per USD (deterministic greedy knapsack). Financial metrics use an 8% discount rate over a 10-year horizon.

These figures are decision-support estimates, not audited accounts — see assumptions & limitations and full factor tables at {{ request.scheme }}://{{ request.get_host }}{% url 'methodology' %}.
