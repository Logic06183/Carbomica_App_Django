CARBOMICA methodology (D3.7)

The optimised scenario uses a greedy knapsack algorithm that ranks interventions by tCO₂e reduced per USD spent — the most cost-effective first — and selects until the budget is exhausted.

Financial metrics use an 8% discount rate (LMIC-appropriate) and a 10-year NPV horizon, aligned with the HIGH Horizons D3.7 reference model (Zenodo: 10.5281/zenodo.12730527).

The three-scenario comparison lets decision-makers see the trade-off between maximum impact (full coverage), lowest cost (fixed budget), and best efficiency (optimised).
